# Fintech Customer-segmentation-Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a **Customer Insights Dashboard** for a fintech company. The dashboard provides a comprehensive understanding of customer demographics, income, tenure, and engagement to help personalize interactions and improve business decision-making.

The dashboard focuses on:
- Customer demographics by age and gender
- Customer loyalty segmentation
- Customer wealth profile
- Customer growth trends over time
- Professional profile of the customer base

---

## Key Metrics

- Total Customers 
- Average Age 
- Average Income 
- Average Tenure 

---

## Visualizations

### Customer by Generation
- Displays the distribution of customers across generations by gender.
- **Observation:** Female customers have a slightly higher representation across most generations.

### Customer Loyalty Segmentation
- Shows the proportion of customers categorized as **Emerging, Legacy, Loyal, and New**.
- **Observation:** 40% of customers are classified as loyal.

### Customer Wealth Profile
- Categorizes customers into **Low, Medium, and High** wealth brackets.
- **Observation:** Majority of low and high-income customers are female, with medium-income fairly balanced.

### Customer Growth Timeline
- Tracks the growth of the customer base from 2015 to 2025.
- **Observation:** Peaks in 2018 and 2022 indicate periods of accelerated growth.

### Professional Profile of Customers
- Displays customer distribution by profession (e.g., Banker, Doctor, Engineer, Retired, Student, Teacher, Trader) and gender.
- **Observation:** Teachers and bankers have the highest representation, with females leading in these categories.

---

## Insights & Recommendations
1. **Loyalty Programs:** With 40% of customers already loyal, implement retention strategies targeting emerging and new customers to increase loyalty.
2. **Target Marketing:** Focus marketing campaigns on female customers in low and high-income brackets, as they are a significant segment.
3. **Professional Segmentation:** Leverage profession-specific campaigns for teachers and bankers, as they form the largest professional groups.
4. **Growth Analysis:** Investigate the drivers of customer growth in 2018 and 2022 to replicate successful strategies in future campaigns.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Excel:** Dashboard creation and visualization
- **Data Sources:** Customer demographic, income, and tenure datasets
- **Analysis Techniques:** Segmentation, trend analysis, and profiling
